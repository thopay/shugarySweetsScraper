instructions: ["Wash and dry the zucchini. Cut away both ends.","Position the grater over a cutter board. With one hand holding the grater and one hand holding the zucchini at one end, apply even pressure and run the zucchini away from you, over the grater. Continue with this motion until only a very small piece of the zucchini remains.","Wash and dry the zucchini. Cut away both ends and cut the zucchini in half lengthwise.  ","Insert the disc for grating into the food processor with the grating holes facing up toward the top of the food prep bowl. Attach the top of the food processor and insert the zucchini in the food chute. Turn on the food processor and use the food pusher to press the zucchini into the grating disc.","For every pound of grated zucchini, place grated zucchini in a colander and add 1 teaspoon of salt to draw out the excess liquid.  ","After about 15-20 minutes, using your hands, squeeze any remaining liquid from the zucchini and transfer to a plate lined with a paper towel."]
